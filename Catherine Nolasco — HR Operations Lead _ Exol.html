About Catherine

I'm an HR Operations professional with 13+ years of experience building the systems, processes, and compliance infrastructure that keep HR running with precision across multi-state, high-growth organizations. My career spans from HR Coordinator to Director of People Operations — growing through every layer of the function so I understand it from the ground up.

For Exol, I bring exactly what this role demands: deep expertise in benefits administration and vendor management, hands-on leave coordination across FMLA, ADA, CFRA, and state-specific plans, and high-volume HRIS data administration across Workday, ADP Workforce Now, and Rippling. I've administered benefits for populations of 200–700+ employees, managed 7 open enrollment cycles end-to-end, and handled ~48 leave cases per year across complex, distributed workforces.

I'm a meticulous operator who takes accuracy seriously — because I know that data integrity in HR systems, correct benefit invoicing, and timely employee escalation resolution aren't just administrative details. They're what employees rely on and what leadership depends on for good decision-making. SHRM-CP certified. Zero I-9 violations across 7 years and multiple RIFs. Ready to bring that same standard to Exol.
